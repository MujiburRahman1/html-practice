⁢h1>Heading Elements</h1>

HTML provides six levels of headings, from <h1> to <h6>. The <h1> tag defines the most important heading, while the <h6> tag defines the least important heading.

Headings are used to create a hierarchy of content on a web page, making it easier for users to navigate and understand the structure of the document.

Paragraph Element

This is a paragraph element. Paragraphs are used to group related sentences together.

HTML paragraphs are defined with the <p> tag. Each paragraph will have a space above and below it by default.

Here is another paragraph to demonstrate how multiple paragraphs look on a page. Each paragraph is separated by a blank line.

Remember, paragraphs are important for readability and structure in your HTML documents.

Nested <p> paragraphs are not valid in HTML, so avoid placing a <p> tag inside another <p> tag.

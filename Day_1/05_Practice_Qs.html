Avengers

Iron Man

Iron Man is a superhero in the Marvel Comics universe. He is the alter ego of Tony Stark,
a wealthy industrialist and genius inventor.

Captain America

Captain America is a superhero in the Marvel Comics universe. He is the alter ego of Steve Rogers,
a frail young man enhanced to the peak of human physicality by an experimental serum in order to aid the United States government's efforts during World War II.

Thor

Thor is a superhero in the Marvel Comics universe. He is the Norse God of Thunder and the son of Odin, the ruler of Asgard.
Thor wields a powerful hammer called Mjolnir, which grants him the ability to fly and control lightning.

Hulk

The Hulk is a superhero in the Marvel Comics universe. He is the alter ego of Bruce Banner,
a brilliant scientist who, after a gamma radiation accident, transforms into the Hulk when he experiences intense anger or stress.
